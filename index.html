About ReaPix

Reaction Images are a staple of the internet. They're scattered across Reddit, Discord, Tiktok, Youtube, and way more. Finding them, however, is not so easy. Most people just save memes that they see or like, and then they go to die in their Downloads folder.

I was kind of tired of this, so I made an archive of all my reaction memes. After a while though, I spent hours on reddit and other sites looking for new images for my new collection. When I hit 1000 images, and Github couldn't show all of them on one repo, I decided to start my collection as a public archive website of images that any interweb dweller could use to their hearts content.

Since the name "Reaction Pic Library by Retrofries" is terrible, I named it ReaPix. You know, a portmanteau of "reaction" and "pictures"... you get it. As of right now, I've reached a grand total of ... images! I'm excited to see how far this can go, and I hope you enjoy!





How to Extract

Extracting the ReaPix Library is easy. If you want the latest collection, simply click "Download Latest Collection" at the top of the page. Doing this will give you a decently sized ZIP file.

Unzip the file and place the Reaction Pics folder in your desired location, like Desktop for example. Once you've done that, you can delete the ZIP file and any other leftovers.

If you're on Mac, I'm so sorry... but pity aside, you can use the built-in Archive Utility to extract it.

Contribute

If you have want to add YOUR OWN reaction image to the ReaPix Library, you can join the Discord with the button at the top. You can post the image in the #recommend channel, and a mod can add it! Because it would be difficult to implement, your image won't be credited with your account. Keep this in mind, and you'll be good to go!
